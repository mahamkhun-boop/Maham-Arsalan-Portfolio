How I approach a brief

  • Structure serves search intent

    Numbered listicles for skimmable, snippet-friendly answers. Narrative essays when a topic needs argument, not a checklist. The format is a decision, not a default.

  • Every link earns its place

    When a piece is written to support link building, the contextual link sits inside a sentence that would exist anyway — never bolted on. Anchor text matches commercial intent without breaking the read.

  • Local and niche targeting

    Geo-modified angles (a renovation piece written for an Indianapolis audience, a plumbing link for a Northwich readership) so content serves both the reader and the client's local SEO goals.

  • E-E-A-T where it matters

    Health and finance content cites real studies, government sources, and named organizations — because YMYL topics get held to a higher bar, by readers and by Google.
